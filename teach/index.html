2025-12-15 Philosophy

On the Process of Making

Making is not a performance. It is a dialogue between intent, material, and constraint.

I don’t start with outcomes. I start with questions: What survives contact with reality?

Process matters because it outlives the artifact. What persists is how decisions are made.
